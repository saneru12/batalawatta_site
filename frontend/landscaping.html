Our Landscaping Process

Landscape architect / design specialist involvement එකෙන් project එක professionally organize කරන step-by-step workflow එක.

6-step real-world process
01

Inquiry & Project Brief

Customer requirement, property type, budget range, garden size, and goals collect කරලා project brief එක build කරනවා.

02

Site Visit & Analysis

Landscape architect / specialist site condition, sun-shade pattern, drainage, circulation, and usable zones analyse කරනවා.

03

Concept Design & Plant Palette

Layout direction, planting character, focal points, practical materials, and plant palette customer requirement එකට match කරලා propose කරනවා.

04

Quotation & Scope Confirmation

Package scope refine කරලා final quotation, timeline, and implementation method customer approval සඳහා share කරනවා.

05

Installation / Execution

Planting, bed shaping, selected hardscape touches, and site execution supervised way එකෙන් complete කරනවා.

06

Handover & Aftercare

Final walkthrough, maintenance instructions, watering guidance, and agreed follow-up support customer ට handover කරනවා.
